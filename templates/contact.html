❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How often is the data updated?
    A: USGS updates lake levels every 15-60 minutes. Our site fetches fresh data on every page load.
  • Q: Is this data official?
    A: Yes! All data comes directly from official USGS and weather.gov APIs. We just display it in an easier format.
  • Q: Can I use this data for my own purposes?
    A: The USGS data is public domain. Our presentation is provided as-is for informational purposes. For critical decisions, always verify with official sources.
  • Q: Do you have a mobile app?
    A: Not yet, but the website is mobile-friendly and works great in your phone's browser. You can add it to your home screen for quick access.
  • Q: Can I donate or support the site?
    A: This is a free community service. No donations needed! Just share the site with fellow lake lovers.
